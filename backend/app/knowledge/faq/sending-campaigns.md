# Sending a bulk SMS campaign

To send a bulk SMS campaign on the Gatum platform:

1. Open the **Campaigns** section in the left sidebar.
2. Click **New campaign** and give it a name.
3. Upload your recipient list as a CSV with a `phone` column, or pick a saved segment.
4. Choose an approved **Sender ID** from the dropdown.
5. Write your message text. The counter shows segments and estimated cost.
6. Click **Send now** or schedule it for later.

Campaigns start sending immediately once submitted. You can pause a running campaign
from the campaign detail page.
