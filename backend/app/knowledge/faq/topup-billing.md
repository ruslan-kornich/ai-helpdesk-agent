# Billing and topping up your balance

The Gatum platform uses a prepaid balance. Each sent message segment is deducted from it.

1. Open the **Billing** section.
2. Click **Top up balance**.
3. Choose an amount and a payment method.
4. After payment confirms, your balance updates automatically.

You can view past invoices and current balance on the Billing overview. If a payment
needs manual confirmation, our finance team will verify the transaction once you submit
the confirmation details.
