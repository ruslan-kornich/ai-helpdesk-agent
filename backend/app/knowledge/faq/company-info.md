# About Gatum

Gatum is an SMS/SMPP messaging platform that lets businesses send transactional and
marketing campaigns, register Sender IDs, top up balance, and track delivery reports.
Customers integrate over the HTTP API or an SMPP connection.

## Support channels

- AI support assistant on Telegram for first-line help and triage.
- Email and ticketing through the support desk for issues that need a human specialist.
- Urgent platform outages are escalated immediately to the L2 support team.

## Working hours

The support team works business hours on weekdays. Messages received outside working
hours still create a ticket and receive a reply once the team is back. The authoritative
working hours and timezone are configured in the admin settings; the values shown in chat
reflect that live configuration.
